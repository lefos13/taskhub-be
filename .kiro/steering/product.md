# TaskHub Backend - Product Overview

TaskHub is a comprehensive project management system designed for Team Leaders and Project Managers to organize tasks, manage teams, track workloads, and monitor deadlines.

## Core Features

- **Task Management**: Create, update, and track tasks with status, priority, deadlines, and manday estimates
- **Project Management**: Organize tasks into projects with status tracking and analytics
- **Team Management**: Assign team members to projects and tasks, track workloads
- **User Management**: Authentication, authorization, and user profiles with role-based access control (RBAC)
- **Time Tracking**: Log hours worked on tasks and calculate consumed mandays
- **Analytics**: Workload distribution, project progress, and team performance metrics
- **Real-time Notifications**: WebSocket-based notifications for deadline alerts and task updates
- **Reporting**: Generate project status reports and export capabilities

## Target Users

- Team Leaders managing multiple projects and team members
- Project Managers tracking deadlines, workloads, and project progress
- Team Members updating task status and logging time

## Business Value

- Centralized task and project organization
- Real-time workload visibility and deadline tracking
- Data-driven project analytics and reporting
- Improved team collaboration and accountability
